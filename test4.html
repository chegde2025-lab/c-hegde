When Should Reserve Tables Be Loaded?

1. What Does Loading Reserve Table Mean?

Loading reserve tables means creating, updating, or recalculating financial liabilities associated with a claim. It ensures that unpaid claim obligations are accurately tracked in the system.

Reserve tables must be loaded whenever financial liability changes.

2. Event-Based Reserve Loading Triggers

Event Load Required? Reason
contactRoleChanged No No financial impact
claimChanged Sometimes If coverage or loss details affect estimate
claimStatusChanged → OPEN Yes Initialize reserve rows
reserveChanged Yes Core financial update
paymentChanged Yes Deduct from outstanding reserve
claimStatusChanged → CLOSED Yes Release remaining reserve
Month-End Close Yes Financial reporting & actuarial review
Claim Reopened Yes Reinitialize or increase reserves

3. Operational Flow Diagram
